---

# 📋 Ingredients
- 🍓 250g of strawberries
- 🥛 250ml of milk
- 150g of yogurt
- 2 tsp of sugar
- 🧊 6 ice cubes

# 🔢 Instructions
1. Clean strawberries and remove stem
2. Add ingredients (except ice) and crush at 1 min/speed 10
3. Add ice and crush at 30 sec/speed 8

# 💡 Ideas
- Add a banana 🍌 for extra creaminess
