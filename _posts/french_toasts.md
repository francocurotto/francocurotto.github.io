# Ingredients 
- 🥚 1 egg
- 🥛 60 ml of milk
- 🍞 4 slices of bread
- 2 ml vanilla (1/2 spoon 🥄)
- pinch of cinnamon

# Instructions
- Mix ingredients in bowl 🥣
- Soak breads in mix and cook in pan with butter 🧈 until brown
- Cut each bread in 4
- Decorate with berries 🫐 and syrup 🥞
